Learn about staying safe online

A few reputable, free places to actually learn about being online responsibly and staying safe.

National Cybersecurity Alliance

Practical, plain-language guidance on passwords, phishing, privacy settings, and general online safety for individuals and families.

ConnectSafely

Guides focused on social media, apps, and platforms which is written for real day-to-day situations rather than abstract policy.

Common Sense Media

Digital wellness and media literacy resources, useful if you're thinking about healthy tech habits more broadly, not just security.

CyberEthos doesn't collect anything you do here! These are just outbound links to independent organizations. The internet is vast and forever growing, but with guidance you can stay always on the right side of it.
