Support & get help

If you're dealing with something online right now (harassment, a privacy violation, bullying), these organizations can actually help. If you're just looking to support this kind of work, they're also worth donating to.

Cyber Civil Rights Initiative

Support for people experiencing image-based abuse and online harassment, plus a crisis helpline.

StopBullying.gov

U.S. government resource for reporting and responding to cyberbullying, aimed at both teens and parents.

National Cybersecurity Alliance

Nonprofit doing the day-to-day work of public cyber safety education. Worth supporting if this app's mission resonates with you.

If you're in immediate danger or crisis, please contact local emergency services or a crisis line directly — these organizations support online safety specifically, not emergencies.
